    Démontrer que les droites $\mathspoint{(\<S1;\<S4;)}$ et $\mathspoint{(\<S2;\<S3;)}$
    sont parallèles entre elles.

    \begin{tikzpicture}[rotate=40,scale=0.7]\bfseries
        \tikzset{every pic/.style={linewidth=.4pt}}
        \coordinate[label=below:\mathspoint{\<S1;}](A)at(0,0);
        \coordinate[label=right:\mathspoint{\<S2;}](B)at(3,0);
        \coordinate[label=\mathspoint{\<S3;}](C)at(3,4);
        \coordinate[label=left:\mathspoint{\<S4;}](D)at(0,2);
        \tkzMarkRightAngle[colored](B,A,D)
        \tkzMarkRightAngle[colored](C,B,A)
        \draw(A)--(B)--(C)--(D)--cycle;
    \end{tikzpicture}
\end{minipage}
